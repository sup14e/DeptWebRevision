Curriculum Design Philosophy

The curriculum of our department is divided into three parts, providing students with a comprehensive learning system through a combination of required core courses, mathematics and foundational science courses, and engineering professional courses. The core required courses consist of university-wide compulsory courses and essential general education courses, including Chinese, English, History, and other general education subjects.

  • Core Required Courses: 23 credits in total, including 9 credits of university-wide required courses and 14 credits of general education courses (comprising both core required and elective courses).
  • Departmental Required Courses: 60 credits
  • Elective Courses: 45 credits
