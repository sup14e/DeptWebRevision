RESEARCH AREAS

The department’s research is organized into four major research groups, structured according to the faculty’s areas of expertise. Senior faculty members guide junior faculty in developing specialized and interdisciplinary research projects. These groups reflect the department’s diverse and dynamic academic development, striving to remain on par with top institutions in Taiwan. The four research groups and their key focuses are as follows:

Artificial Intelligence, Data Science & Multimedia Research Group

  • Video surveillance and automotive vision safety research
  • Machine learning techniques
  • Image processing and video encoding technologies
  • Data analytics methods and applications

Cybersecurity, Networking, Databases & Systems Research Group

  • Development of advanced network communication technologies
  • Exploration of next-generation network applications
  • Construction and application of cryptographic theory
  • Comprehensive information security research and systems development

E-Learning Research Group

  • Integration of computer technology with psychology and education
  • Online learning and collaborative learning systems
  • Design of wireless communication and mobile learning environments
  • Learning simulators and virtual reality environments

Software Engineering & Theoretical Computing Research Group

  • Software and software engineering using conceptual modeling
  • Object-oriented methods and service-oriented software
  • Algorithms
  • Graph theory and optimization
