Graduation Information

Department-Specific Academic Criteria for Early Graduation of Undergraduate Students

  • Students whose overall academic average ranks within the top 20% of their class in the department and whose overall average grade is 75 or above.
  • Students whose conduct grade in each semester is 80 or above.
